The Credit Explained

Section 45B: A Permanent Federal Credit Most Restaurants Miss

The FICA Tip Credit (IRC §45B) allows qualifying employers to recover a portion of the Social Security and Medicare taxes they pay on employee tip income — dollar-for-dollar against their federal tax bill.

Despite being a permanent part of the tax code, the vast majority of eligible restaurants have never claimed it. Not because they don't qualify — but because most CPAs and payroll companies simply don't specialize in this area of recovery.
